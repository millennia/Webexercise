One Summer in Europe

In the winter of 2014, I was sitting down with my family one Friday evening for tea. As we all bickered away about our exhausting week of work and school, we suddenly realized how much we were missing out on life. We all agreed that a family vacation was long over due.
As all of us are very much into learning about history and different cultures around the world, a decision was made that we will make a 2 weeks trip to Europe in the summer of 2014.
The responsibility of planning the trip was taken up by me and my elder daughter and the intense period of extensive research started right there and then for our upcoming Europe trip.
